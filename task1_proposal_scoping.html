Risk & Contingency Approach

Brand Development:

Two full revision cycles are included across all deliverables. The logo and brand guidelines constitute essential deliverables under the base scope. All additional brand materials (social templates, PR assets) will be delivered in editable design formats for client-side customization. Requests beyond two revision rounds or additional design applications will be quoted separately as change orders.

Website Development:

Product catalogue details (names, descriptions, pricing, images, specifications) must be provided and confirmed before design development commences. Any unflagged issues or bugs not identified before the end of Week 14 (execution phase completion) fall outside the base scope and will be addressed under separate support arrangements. Technical integration issues with third-party applications will be resolved through Bullshark-provided alternatives at no additional cost to the client.

Marketing Retainer:

Campaign strategies require time to generate measurable results, and all content must be reviewed and approved two weeks prior to the scheduled publication date. Additional scope requests (extra campaigns, rush projects) may be accommodated either through separate billing or by reducing the monthly content volume proportionally. Monthly hour allocations provide built-in flexibility to adapt to shifting priorities without formal contingency buffers.
