4. RISK ASSESSMENT & MITIGATION

Commercial Risks

Risk 1: Feature Scope Creep

Stakeholders may request new features, lose track of original scope, or cause delays in feedback and testing phases, threatening the 34-week deadline.

Mitigation:

  • Weekly stakeholder steering committee
  • Change freeze enforced during development phases
  • Contingency budget buffer allocated for minor refinements only

Risk 2: Data Security & GDPR Compliance Complications

Different user roles, GDPR standards, and regulatory constraints may impose difficulty and slow down security processes, particularly for minor-dependent accounts.

Mitigation:

  • External legal review of data handling
  • Privacy-by-design principles embedded
  • GDPR compliance checkpoint at each phase

Risk 3: Tight Timeline Requires Intensive Resource Deployment

The compressed timeline induces risk and requires focused team allocation, resource lockdown, and agile execution through all phases to meet delivery commitments.

Mitigation:

  • Dedicated full-time team deployed from week 1
  • Upfront payment to secure team availability
  • Backup developers on retainer for critical path activities
  • Pre-allocated development environment ready before project kickoff

Delivery Risks

Risk 1: UAT Coordination & Platform Demo Education Materials

Organization of educational demos and user manuals within the timeframe may prove challenging, and integration may require additional technical support during UAT phases.

Mitigation:

  • Product expert assigned to maintain and organize product requirements
  • Pre-built training materials and video walkthroughs prepared during Phase 6
  • Staggered UAT schedule with prioritized stakeholder groups
  • Dedicated UAT coordinator assigned from week 20

Risk 2: Stakeholder Alignment Challenges

8 stakeholder groups require administration and may have conflicting priorities, creating coordination complexity and potential requirement conflicts.

Mitigation:

  • Hierarchy-based decision system prioritizing Ministry → Administrators → Educators → Parents
  • Single point of contact (SPOC) designated for each stakeholder group
  • Support and customer care trained for each stakeholder type
  • Fault tracking system with appropriate priority flagging

Risk 3: Third-Party Integration Failures & Security Vulnerabilities

Delayed progress due to national data bottlenecks, security issues, and third-party app integration failures caused by outdated technology or API dependencies.

Mitigation:

  • Pre-integration security audit of all third-party APIs
  • SDK hackathons for essential third-party software integration testing
  • API abstraction layer to isolate dependencies
